The Invitation

A Conclave for the Soul Oil of Bharat

It is our privilege to invite you to the National Edible Oil Conclave 2026, to be held on 19th & 20th September 2026 in Guwahati, Assam.

We are honoured to host this prestigious conclave under the aegis of the Mustard Oil Promotion Council, and sincerely thank COOIT for entrusting us with this responsibility. Your gracious presence and valuable participation will make this landmark event truly memorable.
